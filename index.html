About Lost & Sound

We're Lost & Sound, and we're here to bring all your favorite indie and alternative hits to life when you're ready to go out and have a great time around the Washington DC area.

Think of us as that friend who always has the perfect song for every moment – except we play them live and probably way too loud!

We dig deep into the treasure chest of alternative music, from the moody brilliance of The Cure and Joy Division to the dance-your-heart-out vibes of LCD Soundsystem and Franz Ferdinand. Our setlists are like a greatest hits collection of the bands that made you fall in love with music in the first place.

Come hang out, sing along (we won't judge your voice), and let's celebrate the music that gets us through life.
