About Lost & Sound

We're Lost & Sound, and we're here to bring all your favorite Indie and Alternative hits to life when you're ready to go out and have a great time around the Washington DMV area.

We dig deep into the treasure chest of back catalog of alternative music, from the moody brilliance of The Cure and Joy Division to the dance-your-heart-out vibes of LCD Soundsystem and Franz Ferdinand.

Come hang out, dance, sing along, and let's celebrate the music that gets us through life.
